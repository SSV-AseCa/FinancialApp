# Workflow: Investor's Company Evaluation
- **Actor:** Investor
- **Goal:** Evaluate companies before investing
## Steps
1. Investor searches for companies by name or ticker
2. System searches for company information through the SEC EDGAR API
3. System presents matching companies
4. Investor selects a company of interest
5. System retrieves
	- Financial metrics
	- Most recent filing
	- Historical financial data
6. System present the retrieved financial information
7. Investor evaluates the company based on the information received
