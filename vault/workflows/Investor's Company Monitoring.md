# Workflow:  Investor's Company Monitoring
- **Actor:** Investor
- **Goal:** Monitor companies of interest without owning stocks from them
## Steps
1. Investor searches for a company by name or ticker
2. System presents matching companies
3. Investor selects a company
4. Investor adds the company to the watchlist
5. System stores the company in the investor's watchlist
6. Investor access watchlist
7. Investor requests financial comparison between watched companies
8. System retrieves and presents comparative financial metrics
9. Investor evaluates potential investment opportunities
