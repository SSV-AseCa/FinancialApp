# Workflow: Investor's Stock Operations
- **Actor:** Investor
- **Goal:** Buy or sell stocks based on financial and market data
## Steps
1. Investor selects company
2. Investor reviews
	- Financial information
	- Latest stored market price
3. Investor decides to buy or sell
4. Investor specifies:
	- Operation type
	- Quantity
5. System validates the requested operation
6. System registers the transaction
7. System updates
	- Portfolio positions
	- Portfolio balance
	- Transaction history
8. Investor reviews the updated portfolio
