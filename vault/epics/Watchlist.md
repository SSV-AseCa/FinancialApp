# Watchlist

## Description
Allows investors to monitor companies of interest without necessarily owning their stocks. Investors can maintain a personal watchlist and compare financial metrics across watched companies to identify investment opportunities.

## Goals
- Allow investors to add companies to their watchlist
- Allow investors to remove companies from their watchlist
- Present financial metrics for all watched companies
- Allow investors to compare financial metrics between watched companies

## Stories
- [[Add Company to Watchlist]]
- [[Remove Company from Watchlist]]
- [[View Watchlist Financial Metrics]]
- [[Compare Watchlist Companies]]

## Acceptance Criteria
- Watchlist is personal to each authenticated investor
- Financial metrics for watched companies are retrievable and comparable
- A company can be on the watchlist without the investor holding a position in it
