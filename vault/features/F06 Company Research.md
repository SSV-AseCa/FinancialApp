# Company Research

## Description
Allows investors to discover and evaluate companies before investing. Data is sourced from SEC EDGAR. Covers company search, current financial metrics, recent SEC filings, and historical financial data.

## Goals
- Allow investors to search for companies by name or ticker
- Present financial metrics, SEC filings, and historical data for a selected company
