# Watchlist

## Description
Allows investors to monitor companies of interest without owning their stocks. Investors maintain a personal watchlist and can compare financial metrics across watched companies.

## Goals
- Allow investors to add and remove companies from their watchlist
- Present financial metrics for all watched companies
- Allow investors to compare metrics across watched companies
