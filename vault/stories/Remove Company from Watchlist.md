# Remove Company from Watchlist

## User Story
As an investor, I want to remove a company from my watchlist so that I can clean up companies I'm no longer interested in.

## Acceptance Criteria
- The investor selects a watched company and removes it
- The system removes it from the investor's watchlist and it no longer appears in the watchlist view
- Removing a company not on the watchlist returns an appropriate error
