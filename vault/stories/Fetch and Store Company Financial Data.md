# Fetch and Store Company Financial Data

## User Story

As a system, I want to fetch and store company financial
data and SEC filings from EDGAR so that the
Company Research and Watchlist features have data to display.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Financial metrics and recent filings are retrievable
from EDGAR for any searched company
- Retrieved data is stored and available to the Company Research and Watchlist epics
- EDGAR rate limit is respected at all times
